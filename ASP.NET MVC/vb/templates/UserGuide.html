Outpatient Attendance User Guide (v.0.0.1)
 

 

Dynamic Outpatient Outcome Form

User Guide

Accessing the form

To access the forms first open up Internet Explorer by clicking on the icon.

Once Internet Explorer has been opened type bi.medway.nhs.uk into the address bar and hit enter.

You will be prompted to login. Enter your Trust username and password (the same account you use to login to Windows) and hit enter.

Once you have logged in a list of documents that are available are displayed; click on Outpatient Attendance Form.

This will display a list of options to search for a patient

Searching for a Patient/List

The following options are available to search for a patient.

By Hospital Number

Enter the Hospital Number (you do not need to type in capital letters) and hit enter or click the Search Button.

By NHS Number

Enter the NHS Number and hit enter or click the search button.

By Clinic

Enter the Clinic Code (you do not need to type in capital letters), use the tab key to highlight the Clinic Date field enter the date in dd/mm/yyyy format (you do not need to type the forward slashes). Hit enter or click search

By Pathway

Enter the pathway number (you do not need to type in capital letters) and hit enter or click the Search button.

 

 

Printing a Form

Once the search are displayed click Print. This will open the form as a PDF document.

Once the document has opened click on the Print icon or hit Ctrl+p

Alternatively right-click on the document and select Print… from the options.

Print a Clinic List

When searching by clinic all patients in the list are displayed. Patients can be printed individually by clicking on Print next to the patient (as above).

Alternatively all patients in the list can be printed by clicking Print All.

Once the document has opened click on the Print icon or hit Ctrl+p

Alternatively right-click on the document and select Print… from the options.

Logging Out

The system will keep you signed in for 24hours after which you will be prompted to enter your username and password to continue. If you wish to logout for security reasons click Logout in the top left of the screen.
